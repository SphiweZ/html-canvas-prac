The Darkness Within

Artist: Sphiwe Zungu

Mediums: Hand-sewn Garments and Handmade Zines

Theme: A deeply personal documentation and release of long-lasting sadness through the slow, meticulous process of handcrafting garments and Zines.

Exhibition Overview: The Darkness Within is an intimate exploration of prolonged sadness, expressed through the artist’s hand-sewn garments and handmade Zines. Each stitch, fabric choice, and page reflects a slow, meditative process of confronting and working through personal pain. The exhibition emphasizes craftsmanship, with the artist’s hands bringing to life both the weight of sadness and the potential for release. Visitors are invited to witness and feel the labour involved in creating each piece, immersing themselves in the personal journey toward healing.
